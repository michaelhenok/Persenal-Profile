Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie
One 9-inch pie
30 Min -Prep time
10 Min -Cook time
40 Min- Total
8 Serving

INGREDINTS

  • 1cup white suger
  • 2tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 11/2 cups water
  • 2 Lemon, juiced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 egg
  • 1(9-inch) pie crust
  • 6 tbsp white suger

DIRECTION

1.Preheat Oven:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)

2.Make Lemon Filling :In amedium saucepan ...

  • whiskiy togther 1 vup.sygar.floor.cormstach and salt
  • stir in water lemon juice and flour
  • stir in butter
  • Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually
  • whisk egg yolk mixture
  • Remove from heat
  • Pour filling into baked pastry

3.Make Meringue :In a large glass or metal bowl

4.Bake :Bake in preheated oven for 10min , or until meringue is gold brown
